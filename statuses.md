# Status Codes

> [MDN statuses](https://developer.mozilla.org/ru/docs/Web/HTTP/Status)

---

> [All statuses](https://httpstatuses.com/)

---

## **Informational** [1.xx]

## **Success** [2.xx]:

- success [200]
- created [201]
- no content [204] (ex. _deleting_)

## **Redirection** [3.xx]

- not modified [304]

## **Client Error** [4.xx]

- bad request [400]
- unauthorized [401]
- not found [404]

## **Server Error** [5.xx]

- internal server error [500]
